About Josef Taeubel

An Oregon native that is now located in Minnesota, I enjoy making movies and music about things that interest me. While my primary interest is cinematography, I do also edit the videos as well as the audio for them. My goal is to continue to learn and find my own style and make indie films for a living. Being able to tell a story visually is what I'm striving to do.


I'm looking to do a lot more filming so I can really improve my skills and learn by doing. I would be interested in collaborating with others who have a similar passion for filmmaking and want to create and experiment. Feel free to look at my videos and message me if who have any ideas for projects or talk movies.
